# Overwatch Patch Notes

## May 26, 2026

### Bug Fix Patch

This is a bug fix patch. Replay codes have been wiped.

### Bug Fixes

#### General

- Fixed an issue that prevented users from selecting multiple currency options in the store.
- Fixed an issue that incorrectly calculated Junker Queen's stats in Post Match Accolades.
- Fixed an issue with the time remaining calculation on the Battle Pass.
- Fixed an issue where targets of Nano Boost would not see the appropriate VFX.

---

## May 21, 2026

### Hotfix Patch

This is a hotfix patch. All replay codes from the May 12th patch are still available.

### Stadium Updates

#### Jetpack Cat

**Bouncing Biscuits**

- Bounce healing increased to 35% (up from 25%).

**Ambidexfurous**

- Bonus Shot damage increased to 30% (up from 25%).

**Bell Bomb**

- Explosion Damage increased to 120 (up from 100).

**Tow-Beans**

- Ability Damage per 1% Fuel decreased to 7.5 (down from 10).

### Bug Fixes

#### Stadium

- Fixed an issue where Jetpack Cat's Bell Bomb would stay active when the Power was unequipped.
- Fixed an issue where Juno's Glide Boost cooldown would reset to 0 permanently when using Refresher and Red Promise Regulator.

---

## May 14, 2026

### Balance Hotfix Update

This is a balance hotfix update. All replay codes from the May 12th patch are still available.

### Hero Balance Updates

> These changes aim to re-adjust heroes whose win rates have fallen further than intended since the start of Season 2. We're continuing to monitor mobile heroes adjusted this season to help inform the mid-season changes.

#### Roadhog

**Chain Hook**

- Cooldown reduced from 8 to 7 seconds.

#### Sombra

**Stealth**

- Movement speed bonus reduction while revealed by damage reduced from 50% to 33%.

#### Vendetta

- Health increased from 175 to 200. (Total health increased from 250 to 275.)

### Major Bug Fixes

#### Mizuki

**Katashiro Return**

- Enemies can no longer prevent Mizuki from recalling by standing on Katashiro Return's starting point.

---

## Hero Updates

### Tank

#### Sigma

**Hyper Regeneration – Minor Perk**

- Now restores barrier health only when damaging enemy heroes.

**Experimental Barrier**

- (6v6) Maximum health reduced from 650 to 600.

#### Ramattra

> The Vengeful Vortex perk significantly improved the flexibility of Ravenous Vortex, especially when interacting with airborne targets.

**Ravenous Vortex**

- Vengeful Vortex moved from Perk to Baseline ability.
- Projectile now explodes on contact with the ground.
- Explosion can now be manually detonated while airborne.
- Explosion damage reduced from 50 to 15.

### Damage

#### Emre

**Siphon Blaster**

- Healing conversion reduced from 80% to 60%.

**Cyber Frag**

- Radius of maximum explosion damage reduced from 2 to 1.5 meters.

### Support

#### Baptiste

**Regenerative Burst**

- Instant healing increased from 40 to 50.
- Healing over time increased from 40 to 50.
- Cooldown reduced from 14 to 12 seconds.

---

## General Updates

### Progression

- All heroes now level up faster.

### Console Aim Assist

- Hitscan Auto Assist strength reduced by 4% to more closely match its pre-Season 20 values.
- This change does not affect Legacy Strength Mode.

---

## Competitive Play Updates

- Increased requirement to unlock Competitive Play from 20 wins to 50 wins.
